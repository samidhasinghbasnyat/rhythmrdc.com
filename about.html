~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~ Our Story ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

“Like numerous children encouraged to dance all over the world, I was encouraged by my grandfather to dance since a young age of three or four and before I knew what I was doing, from then to now, dance has become my passion and my form of self-expression through my body movements along the melody of music.

Like myself, I know there are many others like me in the world, inspired by a thing or the other to pursue dance as a hobby or a career itself. Though, the love of dancing perseveres among many, most of us are fearful to display the talent we acquire or the love we have for it and even if we do, we aren’t able to display it in a larger scale whether it may be on a national or an international level, which is why I thought of starting such a competition, to inspire those like me spread all over the world to portray their talents not only within the limitation of their 4-walled room or a school but globally.

This started as mere midnight idea in the back of my mind, which I never thought would actually turn out as big as it is today, without my precious team that has been working endlessly whether it be a weekend or a work day, day or night.

Though it may seem like the creator is the one getting all the credit for the success of everything, I want to take this moment to give credit to all the team members that have believed in this competition and foundation even though it was just a mere idea when it previously started because without them, the idea would still be simmering in the back of my mind with no implementation or impact at all. Thank you to my team once again!”, says Samidha Singh Basnyat.



~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~ Our Purpose ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~


Global Representation

RRDC aims to bring together talented dancers from diverse backgrounds, showcasing their unique skills on an international platform.



Affordable Opportunity

We believe in making dance accessible. By keeping participation costs low, we ensure that talented individuals have the chance to shine, regardless of their financial background.



Judging Process

This competition is more than an event—it's a movement. We are committed to fostering a global dance community that thrives on creativity and mutual respect, with 70% of our donations from the registration fees support our own foundation “Sundar foundation” that shall be dedicated to helping the major issues of countries internationally especially addressing to the issue of lack of entrepreneurial workshops for youngsters and youths that are the future of our world in the further years to come.


~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~ Competition Information ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~


How it Works

Participants submit recorded dance videos, either as solo acts or in teams of up to 15 members.



Judging Process

The competition spans several rounds—starting with a national round, followed by continental, and culminating in an international round that shall be evaluated based on criteria such as dance techniques, expressions, performance quality, and choreography.



Long-Term Vision

Judging will take place 4–5-day span for the national round, with subsequent rounds being judged live on YouTube, accommodating participants from various time zones.



Feedback

In the continental and final round, participants receive personalized feedback, enhancing their learning and growth.
